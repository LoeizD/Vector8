The Founder

Corentin Derbre

Entrepreneur & Real-Time Expert

Corentin Derbre is a serial entrepreneur with a deep-rooted passion for technology and art. From launching a video game studio, Concrete Games, to pioneering software for 3D printing, his career is marked by innovation.

As a consultant, he has lent his real-time rendering expertise to global giants like Microsoft, Temasek, and Industrial Light & Magic (Disney). His artistic work has gained international recognition, and he actively fosters the tech community as the organizer of the Unreal Engine Meetup in Singapore.

With Vector8, Corentin consolidates his vast experience to push the boundaries of real-time technology and create compelling new intellectual properties.
